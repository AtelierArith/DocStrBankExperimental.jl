```
CodeArea(latLo, longLo, codelength)
```

Coordinates of a decoded Open Location Code. The coordinates include the latitude and longitude of the lower left and the code length, which defines the extension of the area.

Attributes:   latitude*low: The latitude of the SW corner in degrees.   longitude*low: The longitude of the SW corner in degrees.   latitude*high: The latitude of the NE corner in degrees.   longitude*high: The longitude of the NE corner in degrees.   latitude*center: The latitude of the center in degrees.   longitude*center: The longitude of the center in degrees.   code_length: The number of significant characters that were in the code.
