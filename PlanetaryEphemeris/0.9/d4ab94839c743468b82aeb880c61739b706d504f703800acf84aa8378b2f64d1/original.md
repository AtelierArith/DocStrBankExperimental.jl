```
meananomaly(x, y, z, u, v, w, m1, m2)
```

Calculate the instantaneous (osculating) mean anomaly for the two body problem defined by the relative position `(x,y,z)` and velocity `(u,v,w)` vectors between two bodies with masses `m1` and `m2`.

See also [`ecanomaly`](@ref) and [`eccentricity`](@ref).
