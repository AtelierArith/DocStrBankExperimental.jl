```
Literal{T}
```

Construct an object that can be used to convert literals to other types. One can use the object in suffix form `1*i8` or `1i8` to perform the conversion.

## Exported constants

  * `i8`: Convert to `Int8`
  * `i16`: Convert to `Int16`
  * `i32`: Convert to `Int32`
  * `u8`: Convert to `UInt8`
  * `u16`: Convert to `UInt16`
  * `u32`: Convert to `UInt32`
