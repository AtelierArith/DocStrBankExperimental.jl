QuapResult

  * `coef`
  * `vcov`
  * `converged`
  * `distr`
  * `params`

where:

coef                            : NamedTuple with parameter MAP estimates, vcov                            : Covariance matric, converged                       : Simple check that multiple chains converged, distr                           : Distributike to sample from (Normal or MvNormal), params                          : Vector of parameter symbols.
