```
hankelh1x(nu, x)
```

Scaled Bessel function of the third kind of order `nu`, $H^{(1)}_\nu(x) e^{-x i}$.

External links: [DLMF 10.2.5](https://dlmf.nist.gov/10.2.5), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bessel_function#Hankel_functions:_H(1)%CE%B1,_H(2)%CE%B1)
