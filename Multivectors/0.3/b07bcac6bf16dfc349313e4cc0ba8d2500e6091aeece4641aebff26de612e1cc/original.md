```
lift(k, m)
```

lift a member (Multivector, KVector, or Blade) of a lower dimensional algebra k into the even subalgebra of another algebra that includes the non-euclidean basis 1-blade m. The bivector generated by lifting is isomorphic to the original member.

example:

```
julia> module f3
         using Multivectors
         @generate_basis("++-")
       end

julia> using .f3

julia> @generate_basis("++") 

julia> lift.([1e₁, 2e₂, 12e₁₂], f3.e₃)
3-element Array{Blade{Int64,2},1}:
  1Main.f3.e₁₃
  2Main.f3.e₂₃
 12Main.f3.e₁₂

julia> ans .* ans
3-element Array{Int64,1}:
    1
    4
 -144

julia> [1e₁, 2e₂, 12e₁₂] .* [1e₁, 2e₂, 12e₁₂]
3-element Array{Int64,1}:
    1
    4
 -144
```
