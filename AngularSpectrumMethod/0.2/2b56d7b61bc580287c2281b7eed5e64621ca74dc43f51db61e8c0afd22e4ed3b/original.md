ShiftedASM(u, λ, Δx, Δy, z, x₀, y₀; expand=true)

> 1. [Kyoji Matsushima, "Shifted angular spectrum method for off-axis numerical propagation," Opt. Express **18**, 18453-18463 (2010)](https://doi.org/10.1364/OE.18.018453)
