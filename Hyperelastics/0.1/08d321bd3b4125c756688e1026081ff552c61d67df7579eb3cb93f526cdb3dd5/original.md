# Model:

$$
W = \frac{\mu}{2}(I_1-3)
$$

# Arguments

  * `type = PrincipalValueForm()`: Sets the form of the strain energy density function. Either `PrincipalValueForm()` or `InvariantForm()

# Parameters:

  * `μ`: Small strain shear modulus
