W = \sum\limits_{i=1}^N\frac{A_i}{2i}(\lambda_1^{2i}+\lambda_2^{2i}+\lambda_3^{2i}-3) + \frac{B_i}{2i}(\lambda_1^{-2i}+\lambda_2^{-2i}+\lambda_3^{-2i}-3)
