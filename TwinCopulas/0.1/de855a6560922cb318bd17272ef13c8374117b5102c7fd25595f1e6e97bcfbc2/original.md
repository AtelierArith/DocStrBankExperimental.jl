The [Upper Frechet-Hoeffding bound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copula_(probability_theory)#Fr%C3%A9chet%E2%80%93Hoeffding_copula_bounds) is defined as

$$
M(u_1,u_2) = \min\{u_1,u_2\}
$$
