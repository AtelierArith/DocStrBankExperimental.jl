cal_humidity

# Arguments

  * `w`    : mix ratio, m*w / m*d
  * `q`    : specific humidity in kg/kg or g/g
  * `Pa`   : surface air pressure
  * `ea`   : actual vapor pressure (kPa)
  * `RH`   : relative humidity, in %
  * `Tair` : air temperature, in degC
  * `Tdew` : dew temperature (in degC)
