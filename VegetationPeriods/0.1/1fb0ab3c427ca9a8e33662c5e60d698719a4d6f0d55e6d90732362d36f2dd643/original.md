LWF_BROOK90()

The end method `LWF_BROOK90` is -for the sake of convenience- a reimplementation of the LWF_BROOK90 VBA (version 3.4) variant of "VonWilpert" (Hammel and Kennel 2001). Their interpretation of von Wilpert (1990) and the somewhat lower precision of VBA was mimicked.
