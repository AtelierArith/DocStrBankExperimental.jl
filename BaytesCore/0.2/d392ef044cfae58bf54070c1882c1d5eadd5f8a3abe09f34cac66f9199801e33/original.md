```julia
shuffle_forward!(
    val,
    buffer_val,
    ancestor,
    buffer_ancestor,
    lookback,
    iterₘₐₓ
)

```

Sort `val` elements from `iterₘₐₓ` up until 'lookback' according to `ancestor` indices at `iterₘₐₓ`. `ancestor` at `iterₘₐₓ - lookback' will be adjusted accordingly.
