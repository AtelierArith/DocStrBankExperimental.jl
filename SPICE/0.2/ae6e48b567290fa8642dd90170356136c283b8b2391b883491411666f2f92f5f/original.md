```
rquad(a, b, c)
```

Find the roots of a quadratic equation.

### Arguments

  * `a`: Coefficient of quadratic term
  * `b`: Coefficient of linear term
  * `c`: Constant

### Output

  * `root1`: Root built from positive discriminant term
  * `root2`: Root built from negative discriminant term
