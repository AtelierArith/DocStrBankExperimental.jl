```
inrypl(vertex, dir, plane)
```

Find the intersection of a ray and a plane.

### Arguments

  * `vertex`, `dir`: Vertex and direction vector of ray
  * `plane`: A plane

### Output

  * `nxpts`: Number of intersection points of ray and plane
  * `xpt1`, `xpt2`: Intersection points
