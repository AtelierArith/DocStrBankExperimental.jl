```
el2cgv(ellipse)
```

Convert an ellipse to a center vector and two generating vectors. The selected generating vectors are semi-axes of the ellipse.

### Arguments

  * `ellipse`: An ellipse

### Output

Returns the center and semi-axes of ellipse.
