Defines different one-dimensional FFT-based transforms.

The transforms are all subtypes of an [`AbstractTransform`](@ref) type.

When possible, the names of the transforms are kept consistent with the functions exported by [`AbstractFFTs.jl`](https://juliamath.github.io/AbstractFFTs.jl/stable/api/) and [`FFTW.jl`](https://juliamath.github.io/FFTW.jl/stable/fft/).
