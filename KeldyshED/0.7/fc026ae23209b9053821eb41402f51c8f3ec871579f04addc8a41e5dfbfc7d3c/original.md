Compute a reduced equilibrium density matrix

$$
\hat\rho_{target} = \mathrm{Tr}_{env}[e^{-\beta\hat H}] / Z
$$

at an inverse temperature $\beta$.

The Hamiltonian $\hat H$ acts in a direct product of [`Hilbert spaces`](@ref KeldyshED.Hilbert.FullHilbertSpace) $H_{target} \otimes H_{env}$, while the resulting reduced matrix acts in $H_{target}$ spanned by all fermionic Fock states generated by the [`set of indices`](@ref KeldyshED.Hilbert.SetOfIndices) `target_soi`.
