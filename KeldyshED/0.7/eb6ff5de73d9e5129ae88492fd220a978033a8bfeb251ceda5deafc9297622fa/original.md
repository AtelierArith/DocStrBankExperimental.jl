```julia
reduced_evolution_operator(
    ed::KeldyshED.EDCore,
    target_soi::KeldyshED.Hilbert.SetOfIndices,
    grid::Keldysh.AbstractTimeGrid
) -> Keldysh.GenericTimeGF{ComplexF64}

```

Compute a reduced evolution operator

$$
\hat S_{target}(t, t') =
\mathrm{Tr}_{env}\left[\exp\left(-i \int_{t'}^{t} \hat H d\bar t\right)\right]
$$

on a given contour time grid.

The Hamiltonian $\hat H$ acts in a direct product of [`Hilbert spaces`](@ref KeldyshED.Hilbert.FullHilbertSpace) $H_{target} \otimes H_{env}$, while the resulting reduced evolution operator acts in $H_{target}$ spanned by all fermionic Fock states generated by the [`set of indices`](@ref KeldyshED.Hilbert.SetOfIndices) `target_soi`.
