```
SquareSet(ss::Vararg{Square})
```

Construct a square set with the provided squares.

# Examples

```julia-repl
julia> SquareSet(SQ_A1, SQ_A2, SQ_A3, SQ_A4)
SquareSet:

 -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
 -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
 -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
 #  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
 #  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
 #  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
 #  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
```
