```
DiagonalBFGS(d)
```

A diagonal approximation of the BFGS update inspired by  Marnissi, Y., Chouzenoux, E., Benazza-Benyahia, A., & Pesquet, J. C. (2020).  Majorize–minimize adapted Metropolis–Hastings algorithm. https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/9050537. 

# Arguments

  * `d::AbstractVector`: initial diagonal approximation.
