```
palette(colors, [n]; rev = false, alpha = nothing)
```

Construct a `ColorPalette`. Accepts symbols for Colorschemes.jl `ColorScheme`s, `ColorScheme`s, vectors of colors, `ColorGradient`s and `ColorPalettes`. If 'n' is provided, `n` colors are chosen equidistantly from the colorscheme. If `rev` is `true` colors are reversed.
