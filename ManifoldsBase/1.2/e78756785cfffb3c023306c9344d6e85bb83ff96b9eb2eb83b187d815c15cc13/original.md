```
DefaultBasis{ğ”½,VST<:VectorSpaceType}
```

An arbitrary basis of vector space of type `VST` on a manifold. This will usually be the fastest basis available for a manifold.

The type parameter `ğ”½` denotes the [`AbstractNumbers`](@ref) that will be used as coefficients in linear combinations of the basis vectors.
