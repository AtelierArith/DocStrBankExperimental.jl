Invoked when an [`aws_event_stream_message`](@ref) is flushed to the IO interface. When error_code is AWS_ERROR_SUCCESS the write happened successfuly. Regardless, message is held from the [`aws_event_stream_channel_handler_write_message`](@ref)() call and should likely be freed in this callback. If error_code is non-zero, the channel will be shutdown immediately after this callback returns.
