Tracy-Widom distribution

The probability distribution of the normalized largest eigenvalue of a random matrix with iid Gaussian matrix elements of variance 1/2 and mean 0.

The cdf of Tracy-Widom is given by

$F_β (s) = lim_{n→∞} Pr(√2 n^{1/6} (λ_max - √(2n) ≤ s)$

where β = 1, 2, or 4 for the orthogonal, unitary, or symplectic ensembles.
