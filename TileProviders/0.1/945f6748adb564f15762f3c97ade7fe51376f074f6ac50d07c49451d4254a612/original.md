Stadia(variant)

[`Provider`](@ref) for Stadia tiles.

(C) Stadia Maps (C) OpenMapTiles (C) OpenStreetMap contributors

# Arguments

  * `variant`: :AlidadeSmooth, :AlidadeSmoothDark, :OSMBright, :Outdoors, :StamenToner, :StamenTonerBackground, :StamenTonerLines, :StamenTonerLabels, :StamenTonerLite, :StamenWatercolor, :StamenTerrain, :StamenTerrainBackground, :StamenTerrainLabels and :StamenTerrainLines, with a default of AlidadeSmooth.
