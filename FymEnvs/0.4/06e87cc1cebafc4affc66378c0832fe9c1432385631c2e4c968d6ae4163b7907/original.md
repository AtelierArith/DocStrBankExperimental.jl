module FymEnvs

`FymEnvs` is developed by research project `NRF` of `FDCL` (fligth dynamics and control laboratory) in `SNU` (seoul national university).

The origin version of this package, `Fym`, has been developed in `Python`. This is a Julia version of the `Fym`.

In this module, some parts follows the convention of `Fym`, e.g., `BaseEnv` and `BaseSystem`.
