Potential of *direct* screening for the spectator electron in `orbit 1` by the screening  electron in `orbit2` with (reduced) radial wavefunction `P2`.

$$
U_{F}(u_{\kappa},u_{\kappa^{\prime}};\rho)
={\textstyle \sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty}}a^{k}(lm_{l};l^{\prime}m_{l^{\prime}})U_{F}^{k}(nl;\rho).
$$
