The Next Subvolume Method for spatial jump process simulations. Usually slower than `DirectCRDirect`. Uses an indexed priority queue tree structure to determine where on the grid/graph the next jump occurs, and then the `Direct` method to determine which jump at the given location occurs.

Elf, Johan and Ehrenberg, M, Spontaneous separation of bi-stable biochemical systems into spatial domains of opposite phases,Systems Biology, 2004 vol. 1(2) pp. 230-236. doi:10.1049/sb:20045021
