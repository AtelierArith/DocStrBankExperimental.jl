```
PathSearchSolution(status, plan)
PathSearchSolution(status, plan, trajectory)
PathSearchSolution(status, plan, trajectory, expanded,
                   search_tree, search_frontier, search_order)
```

# フィールド

  * `status`
  * `plan`
  * `trajectory`
  * `expanded`
  * `search_tree`
  * `search_frontier`
  * `search_order`
