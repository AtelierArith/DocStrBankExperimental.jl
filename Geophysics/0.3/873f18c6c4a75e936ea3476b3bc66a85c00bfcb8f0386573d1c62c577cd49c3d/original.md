```
FluidState{f}
```

Thermodynamic state of fluid `f` at temperature `T` and pressure `P`. Induces derived values `fluid`, `temperature`, `pressure`, `density`, `specificvolume`, `kinematic`, `heatcapacity`, `thermaldiffusivity`, `elasticity`, `specificimpedance`, `intensity`, and values associated with `f::AbstractMole` derivations.
