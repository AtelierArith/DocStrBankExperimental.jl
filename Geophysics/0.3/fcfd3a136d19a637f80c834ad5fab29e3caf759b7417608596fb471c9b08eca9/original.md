```
Planet{f,a,t,Gm}
```

Celestial `Planet` ellipsoidal reference body with `flattening` of `f`, `semimajor` radius of `a`, sidereal `period` of `t`, and standard `gravitation` parameter `Gm`. Further derived parameters include `semiminor`, `frequency`, `angularfrequency`, `eccentricity`, `eccentricity2`, `lineareccentricity`, `aspectratio`, `oblateness`, `dynamicformfactor`, `secondzonalharmonic`, `latitudegeodetic`, `latitudegeocentric`, `latitudeparametric`, `radius`, `radiusgeodetic`, `gravity`, `gravitygeodetic`, and `gravitycomponents`.
