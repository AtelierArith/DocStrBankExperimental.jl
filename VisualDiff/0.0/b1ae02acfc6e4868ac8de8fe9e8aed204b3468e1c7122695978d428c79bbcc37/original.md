Scroll_list(w,list;rows=getmaxy(w),cols=getmaxx(w),begx=0,begy=0,nbcols=1)

Declares  a `Scroll_list` which shows a scrolling  list in window `w` in an area of top left corner `begx,begy` of height `rows` and width `cols` where `cols` are divided in `nbcols` logical columns in which to show part of the list.
