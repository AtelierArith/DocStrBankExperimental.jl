RKMilGeneral: Nonstiff Method RKMilGeneral(;interpretation=SciMLBase.AlgorithmInterpretation.Ito, ii*approx=IILevyArea() An explicit Runge-Kutta discretization of the strong order 1.0 Milstein method for general non-commutative noise problems. Allows for a choice of interpretation between SciMLBase.AlgorithmInterpretation.Ito and SciMLBase.AlgorithmInterpretation.Stratonovich. Allows for a choice of iterated integral approximation. Default: ii*approx=IILevyArea() uses LevyArea.jl to choose optimal algorithm. See Kastner, F. and Rößler, A., arXiv: 2201.08424 Kastner, F. and Rößler, A., LevyArea.jl, 10.5281/ZENODO.5883748, https://github.com/stochastics-uni-luebeck/LevyArea.jl
