Kloeden, P.E., Platen, E., Numerical Solution of Stochastic Differential Equations. Springer. Berlin Heidelberg (2011)

RKMil: Nonstiff Method An explicit Runge-Kutta discretization of the strong order 1.0 Milstein method. Defaults to solving the Ito problem, but RKMil(interpretation=SciMLBase.AlgorithmInterpretation.Stratonovich) makes it solve the Stratonovich problem. Only handles scalar and diagonal noise.
