標準変数属性のリスト。

これらのいくつかはnetCDF COARDS/CF規約に従っています：

COARDS:[https://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/coards-netcdf-conventions](https://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/coards-netcdf-conventions)

  * units   (可能な限りUnidataのudunitsパッケージに従うべきです [https://docs.unidata.ucar.edu/udunits/current/](https://docs.unidata.ucar.edu/udunits/current/))
  * long_name

CF規約: [https://cfconventions.org/cf-conventions/cf-conventions.html#*description*of*the*data](https://cfconventions.org/cf-conventions/cf-conventions.html#_description_of_the_data)

  * standard_name   [http://cfconventions.org/Data/cf-standard-names/current/src/cf-standard-name-table.xml](http://cfconventions.org/Data/cf-standard-names/current/src/cf-standard-name-table.xml)
