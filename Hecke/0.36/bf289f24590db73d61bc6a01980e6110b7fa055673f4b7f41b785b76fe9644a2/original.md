```
scale(g::HermLocalGenus, i::Int) -> Int
```

Given a local genus symbol `g` for hermitian lattices over $E/K$ at a prime $\mathfrak p$ of $\mathcal O_K$, return the $\mathfrak P$-valuation of the scale of the `i`th Jordan block of `g`, where $\mathfrak P$ is a prime ideal of $\mathcal O_E$ lying over $\mathfrak p$.
