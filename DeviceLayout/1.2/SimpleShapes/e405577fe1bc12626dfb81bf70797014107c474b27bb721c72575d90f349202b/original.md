```
simple_tee(w1, h1; w2=h1, h2=w1)
```

Parameters are named in typical handwritten stroke order (like `cross`, vertical stem first). Note that `h1` is the full height of the T.

```
_    _______w2________
|   |▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓| h2
|          |▓▓▓▓|
h1         |▓▓▓▓|
|          |<w1>|
v          |▓▓▓▓|
             ^ (x,y) = (0,0) at center of baseline of T.
```
