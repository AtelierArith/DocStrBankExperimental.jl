# Variables

p ∈ R^3: inertial position v ∈ R^3: inertial velocity R ∈ so(3): inertial-to-body rotation matrix (R_I2B) ω ∈ R^3: angular rate of body in inertial frame u ∈ R^4: [f, M...] where f ∈ R: total thrust, M ∈ R^3: moment
