```
Bar(L, S, E, ρ)
```

Structure containing the data of a homogeneous and isotropic longitudinal bar

**Constructor parameters**

  * `L`: Length [m]
  * `S`: Cross-section area [m²]
  * `E`: Young's modulus [Pa]
  * `ρ`: Mass density [kg/m³]

**Fields**

  * `L`: Length [m]
  * `m`: Line mass [kg/m]
  * `D`: Stiffness coefficient [Pa]
