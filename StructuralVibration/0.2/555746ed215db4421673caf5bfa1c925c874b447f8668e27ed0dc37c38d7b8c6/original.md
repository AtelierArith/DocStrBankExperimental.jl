```
Rod(L, I, J, G, ρ)
```

Structure containing the data of a homogeneous and isotropic torsional bar

**Constructor parameters**

  * L: Length [m]
  * I: Second-moment of area [m⁴]
  * J: Torsion constant [m⁴]
  * G: Shear modulus [Pa]
  * ρ: Mass density [kg/m³]

**Fields**

  * `L`: Length [m]
  * `m`: Line mass [kg/m]
  * `D`: Stiffness coefficient [Pa]
