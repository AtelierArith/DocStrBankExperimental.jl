```
XPRS_TUNERHISTORY
```

Tuner: Whether to reuse and append to previous tuner results of the same problem. (integer)

Default value: `2`

Values: 0 : Discard any previous tuner results. 1 : Append new results to the previous tuner results, but do not reuse them. 2 : Reuse the previous results and append new results to it.
