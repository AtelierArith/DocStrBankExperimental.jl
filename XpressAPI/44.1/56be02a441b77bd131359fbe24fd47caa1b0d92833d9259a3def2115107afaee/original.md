Presolve: convert a linear or quadratic objective function into an objective transfer constraint (integer)

Default value: `-1`

Values: -1 : Automatically determined. 0 : Disable reformulation. 1 : Move only the quadratic part of the objective into a constraint. 2 : Move both the linear and quadratic parts of the objective into a constraint.
