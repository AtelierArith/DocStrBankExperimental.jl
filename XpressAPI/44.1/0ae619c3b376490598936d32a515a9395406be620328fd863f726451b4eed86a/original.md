Presolve: Determines the level of dominated row removal reductions to perform when presolving a problem. (integer)

Default value: `-1`

Values: -1 : Automatically determined. 0 : Disabled. 1 : Cautious strategy. 2 : Medium strategy. 3 : Aggressive strategy. All candidate row combinations will be considered.
