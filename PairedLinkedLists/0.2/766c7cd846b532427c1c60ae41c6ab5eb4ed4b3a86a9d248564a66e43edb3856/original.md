PairedLinkedLists.jl provides implementations for [doubly-linked lists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linked_list#Doubly_linked_list)  and [skip lists](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skip_list), as well as "paired lists" that also contain inter-list links between nodes in two separate lists.
