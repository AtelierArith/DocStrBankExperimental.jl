```
batch()
```

provide batch calculation of the `geometrical efficiency` based on the information provided  by the **`CSV`** files by default located in **`/home/terasaki/GeoEfficiency`**.

results are saved on a **`CSV`**  file(s) named after the detector(s). the **`CSV`**  file(s)  by default found in **`/home/terasaki/GeoEfficiency/results`**, also a log of the results are displayed on the `console`.

**for more information on batch refer to [`batchInfo`](@ref).**
