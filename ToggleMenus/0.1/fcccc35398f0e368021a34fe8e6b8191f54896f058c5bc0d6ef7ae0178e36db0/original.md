ToggleMenus

The `ToggleMenus` module provides a TerminalMenu where options may be toggled through several states.  A template is constructed with a [`ToggleMenuMaker`](@ref), which is used to construct a [`ToggleMenu`](@ref).  The result is passed to [`TerminalMenus.request`](@extref `REPL.TerminalMenus.request`) for display in the REPL.
