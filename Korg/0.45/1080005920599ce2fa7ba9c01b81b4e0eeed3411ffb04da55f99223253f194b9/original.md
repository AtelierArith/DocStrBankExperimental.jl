The GALAH [DR 3](https://www.galah-survey.org/dr3/overview/) linelist (also used for [DR 4](https://github.com/svenbuder/GALAH_DR4)). It ranges from roughly 4,675 Å to 7,930 Å. This linelist is based on, but distinct from [Heiter 2021](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021A%26A...645A.106H/). See [Buder et al. 2021](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021MNRAS.506..150B%2F/abstract) for details.
