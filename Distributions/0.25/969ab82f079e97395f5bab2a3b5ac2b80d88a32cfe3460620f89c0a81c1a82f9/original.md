```
LogUniform(a,b)
```

A positive random variable `X` is log-uniformly with parameters `a` and `b` if the logarithm of `X` is `Uniform(log(a), log(b))`. The *log uniform* distribution is also known as *reciprocal distribution*.

```julia
LogUniform(1,10)
```
