Convert a Julian Date to the equivalent Gregorian calendar date representation of the same instant in time.

# Aguments:

  * `jd::Real`: Julian Date of Epoch

Returns:

  * `year::Int`: Year
  * `year::Int`: Month
  * `year::Int`: Day
  * `hour::Int`: Hour
  * `minute::Int`: Minute
  * `second::Float64`: Seconds
  * `microsecond::Float64`: Nanoseconds
