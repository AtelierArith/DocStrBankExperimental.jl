Convert a Gregorian calendar date to the equivalent Modified Julian Date representation of that time instant.

# Aguments:

  * `year::Integer` Year
  * `year::Integer` Month
  * `year::Integer` Day
  * `hour::Integer` Hour
  * `minute::Integer` Minute
  * `second::Real` Seconds
  * `nanoseconds::Real` Nanoseconds

Returns:

  * `mjd::Float64` Modified Julian Date of Epoch
