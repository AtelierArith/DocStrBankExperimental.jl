Convert a Gregorian calendar date to the equivalent Julian Date representation of that time instant.

# Aguments:

  * `year::Integer`: Year
  * `year::Integer`: Month
  * `year::Integer`: Day
  * `hour::Integer`: Hour
  * `minute::Integer`: Minute
  * `second::Real`: Seconds
  * `nanoseconds::Real`: Nanoseconds

Returns:

  * `mjd::Float64`: Julian Date of Epoch
