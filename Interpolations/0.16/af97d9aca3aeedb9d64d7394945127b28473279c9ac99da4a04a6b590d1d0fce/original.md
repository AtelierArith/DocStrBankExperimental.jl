```
FritschButlandMonotonicInterpolation
```

Monotonic interpolation based on [Fritsch & Butland (1984), "A Method for Constructing Local Monotone Piecewise Cubic Interpolants", doi:10.1137/0905021](@cite Fritsch1984).

Faster than FritschCarlsonMonotonicInterpolation (only requires one pass) but somewhat higher apparent "tension".
