```
destruct(v::Array{<:Tuple,N})
```

Destructure an array of tuples to a tuple of arrays. Works for tuples with elements of varying types.

## Examples

```julia-repl
julia> f(a, b) = a+b, a*b, a-b;
julia> v = f.(rand(3,1), rand(1,4));
julia> x, y, z = destruct(v);
julia> x
```
